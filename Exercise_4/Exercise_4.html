Off Jumpol Adulkittiporn

จุมพล อดุลกิตติพร, also known as Off (ออฟ).
D.O.B: 20 January 1991 in Bangkok, Thailand.

He is a Thai actor and host

Jumpol began working in the entertainment industry as host of the 2013 TV program Five Live Fresh (ไฟว์ไลฟ์). After the show, he debuted as an actor with a guest role in the 2013 Thai drama series Hormones, and got his first main role in the 2014 series Room Alone.

Education

Off Jumpol completed his secondary education in Wat Rajabopit School. He graduated with a bachelor's degree from the Faculty of Information and Communication Technology in Silpakorn University.
